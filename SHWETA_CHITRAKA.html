Medicinal Properties

Identification Features

1) Herbaceous plant with erect stem
2) Flowers are white in colour and are borne in axillary and terminal elongated spikes.
3) The mucilaginous glands aid in trapping insects and fruit dispersal by animal
4) Fruits are green coloured with sticky hairs when young and become dark brown when mature.
