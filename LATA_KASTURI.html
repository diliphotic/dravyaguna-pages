Vernacular Names

HINDI : Kasturi Bhendi MARATHI : Kasturi Bhed ENGLISH NAME : Musk mallow LOCAL NAME : Kastuti bende

Parts Used

Bija

Medicinal Properties

1) Vrishya
2) Chedana
3) Trishnahara
4) Chakshushya

Formulations

1) Kasturyadi gutika
2) Manasamitra gutika
3) Brahmi vati
4) Bala taila

Identification Features

1) Leaves are palmately lobed, hairy on both surfaces.
2) Stem clothed with long hairs.
3) Seed odour resembles musk.
4) Flowers yellow with purple at centre.
