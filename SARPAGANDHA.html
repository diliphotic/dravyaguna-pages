Vernacular Names

HINDI :Chota Chand MARATHI : Adkayi ENGLISH NAME : Snake dog bane LOCAL NAME : Sarpagandha

Parts Used

Root (mula)

Medicinal Properties

1) Nidrajanaka
2) Vranahara
3) Krumihara
4) Vishamajwara

Formulations

1) Sarpagandhaghana vati
2) Sarpagabdhadi churna

Identification Features

1) Leaves are large in whorls.
2) It has a strong odour because of this the snakes wont stay near the plant.
3) Fruits are tiny, oval, fleshy which turn a shine purple black when ripe.
